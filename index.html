About the Studio

We Build Worlds
That Feel Real

Campass Studios is an independent game asset studio specializing in cinematic-quality environments built in Unreal Engine. Every asset is handcrafted with obsessive attention to detail — from the PBR textures on ancient stone to the volumetric torchlight flickering through jungle ruins.

Our work is available on Fab, Epic Games' official marketplace, where developers and creators can instantly drop our assets into their own projects.
